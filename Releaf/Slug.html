Overview

Provides common methods for finding object by slug. Also overrides to_html method.

Simply add this line to your models

include Releaf::Slug

or

ActiveRecord::Base.send(:include, Releaf::Slug)

to some initializer (config/initializers/releaf.rb for example). Then use find_object or find_object! instead of find
